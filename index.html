How to use La Carta

What is La Carta?

Point your phone at any restaurant menu — in any language — and La Carta translates it instantly, describes every dish in plain English, and helps you decide what to order.

Scanning a menu
  1. Tap Analyse Menu on the home screen
  2. Take a photo of the menu — then tap Translate Menu
  3. Need to cover a bigger menu? Tap Add another photo first (up to 3 photos)
  4. The app translates every dish and gives you a one-sentence description
  5. Tap any dish to see it in full with larger text

Good lighting makes a big difference. Try to get the whole menu, or one clear section, in the frame.

Dish images

Tap any dish on the menu, then tap See a photo of this dish to see a picture of it.

Saving and sharing

After a translation, tap Save (top right) to save the menu under a restaurant name. Tap Share to send it to someone.

Saved menus appear on the home screen under 📚 Saved menus.

Your profile

Tap the 👤 icon (top right) to personalise the app. Everything is optional.

  • Language — get results in your own language rather than English
  • Nationality — the app uses food comparisons you'll actually recognise
  • Dietary requirements — the app flags any dish that may conflict with your needs
  • Food preferences — tell the app what you enjoy and a Recommend something for me button will appear after each scan
Dietary flags are based on AI interpretation of menu text only. Always confirm your requirements directly with restaurant staff.
